Classic Tomato Soup

A simple and delicious recipe for a comforting classic.

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 120ml heavy cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, and sugar. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it cook for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. If using an immersion blender, blend the soup directly in the pot until smooth. If using a regular blender, carefully transfer the soup in batches and blend until smooth, then return it to the pot.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream (if using) and heat through, but do not boil.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot.
